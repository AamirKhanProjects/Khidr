Design v4 · de-vibecoded

Flat, very dark, no pills or dots

The look stripped back to something more premium, plus the onboarding flow and bottom-nav icons.

Removed: all gradients (flat fills only), every pill shape, the decorative emerald dots, the middot separators between words.
Changed: background is now near-black green; the wordmark is clean; topic selection uses checkboxes not pills; the feed filter uses underlined text not pills.
Added: a bottom navigation with clear labelled icons (Today, Feed, Reps, Impact), a 3-step onboarding, and a short coachmark tutorial.
