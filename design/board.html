01

Information architecture

Four tabs plus a first-run setup. Everything points at one verb: act on the story in front of you, routed to your own MP.

First run

Setup

  • Mission & how it works, in plain language. No assumed knowledge.
  • Postcode once. Resolve your MP (postcodes.io + Parliament Members API). Stays on device.
  • Pick topics. Gaza, Sudan, Kashmir, domestic civil liberties, etc.
  • Notifications. Opt in to time-sensitive vote alerts only.
Tab 1

Today

  • The lead. The single most important story of the day.
  • Priority action. If a vote is imminent, it sits at the top.
  • 2–3 key items beneath. A digest, not a firehose.
Tab 2

Feed

  • Full curated list. Hairline-separated, editorial.
  • Filter by topic (underline tabs).
  • Each item carries Email + WriteToThem + Call inline.
Tab 3

Your MP

  • Who represents you in Parliament, with contact.
  • Why they matter now (on the relevant committee, or a likely rebel).
  • Contact your MP without a story attached.
Tab 4

Impact

  • Your activity (emails sent, calls made).
  • Outcome loop: how the vote went, how your MP voted.
  • Aggregate: constituent volume this week.
Top-right

More

  • Sources & who curates. Trust layer.
  • How we are funded. Donations / grants.
  • Settings, postcode, notifications, accessibility.

05

What to build, and when

UK first, legally clean. Borrow the MP-routing and contact layer, then build outward.

Now / v1 launch

The core

  • Curated feed + Today digest, your own blurbs, link-out.
  • Knowledge layer: plain-language explainers, no assumed knowledge.
  • MP routing: postcodes.io + UK Parliament Members API. Free, no key.
  • Three send modes: your own email (mailto), WriteToThem, and a call. Khidr never transmits.
  • Bill attached via the UK Bills API.
  • Trust page: sources, who curates, funding.
Next / earns trust

The loop

  • Call-anxiety reducers: what-to-expect line, after-hours voicemail prompt.
  • Outcome follow-up: how the vote went, how your MP voted (Commons Votes API).
  • Impact screen: your activity + aggregate constituent volume.
  • "Why your MP matters now": committee / likely-rebel flags.
  • AI Assist button in the composer.
  • Contact-in day + time-sensitive push for imminent votes, sparingly.
Later / if it earns it

The reach

  • MP accountability scorecard over time (non-partisan).
  • Devolved & local: MSPs, MSs, MLAs, councillors.
  • US module (same shell), once a US lead clears the legal gate.
  • Shareable action cards (growth loop).
  • Accessibility pass (screen-reader parity with 5 Calls).
  • Localisation (Arabic, Urdu, etc.).
