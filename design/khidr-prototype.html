How it works

1

Read what affects you

A short daily brief and a fuller feed, written plainly. No jargon, no firehose.

What we never do

·

We never send for you

Every message leaves from your own email or phone. We only open the door.

·

No account, ever

No sign-up, no login, no profile of you on any server.

·

It stays on your phone

Your postcode and your activity live on this device. Nothing is uploaded.

WriteToThem is a trusted, independent service that delivers your message to your MP. You write and send it there, in your own words.

Why we hand off, not type here

We never send on your behalf. WriteToThem handles delivery, confirms it reached your MP, and blocks identical mass messages, so a personal note gets through.

Notes. Palette, type and 5px corners match the locked system. No AI: you write from a light scaffold, and the send button stays disabled until you make it your own (a nudge to personal messages, visible in the UI). The MP record is factual-only (no score, no verdict), keeping the platform posture clean. Smooth crossfade between screens; sheets slide up. Demo MP is the real, verified member for the demo postcode.
